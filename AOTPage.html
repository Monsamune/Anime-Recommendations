Attack on Titan - Shingeki no Kyojin

Created by Hajime Isayam - Animated by Studio WIP / Studio MAPPA

Action Fiction/Dark Fantasy/Apocalyptic and Post-Apocalyptic Fiction

When man-eating Titans first appeared 100 years ago, humans found safety behind massive walls that stopped the giants in their tracks. But the safety they have had for so long is threatened when a colossal Titan smashes through the barriers, causing a flood of the giants into what had been the humans' safe zone. During the carnage that follows, soldier Eren Jaeger sees one of the creatures devour his mother, which leads him to vow that he will kill every Titan. He enlists some friends who survived to help him, and that group is humanity's last hope for avoiding extinction at the hands of the monsters.

Where to Watch

Attack on Titan is available to watch on Hulu, Youtube TV, Tubi, Funimation, and Crunchyroll

Hulu

Hulu offers a wide variety of monthly subscription options, a $5.99 option for Hulu, $11.99 for Hulu with no ads, $65.99 for Hulu + Live TV, and $70.99 for Hulu with no Ads + Live TV

Youtube TV

Youtube TV is a cable alternative, so it's price is set at $65 a month. It may not be as appealing as the other options, but definitely an option to consider if you are looking for a cable alternative

Tubi

Tubi only has the first season of Attack on Titan available of the current 4 seasons, but you can watch it for free, which may be a good option if you want to check it out without comitting to a service

Funimation

Funimation has a few monthly subscription plans, a $5.99 premium plan, a $7.99 premium plus plan, which gives access to exclusive deals and offline streaming, and a $99.99 yearly plan, which is a bundle on the premium plus plan but also includes yearly gifts/deals.

Crunchyroll

Crunchyroll has 3 monthly subscription plans, a $7.99 "Fan" plan, a $9.99 "Mega Fan" plan, which allows for offline viewing and 4 concurrent streams, and a $14.99 "Ultimate Fan" plan that allows for member-only exclusive rewards and more concurrent streams
